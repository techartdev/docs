---
title: "Share links and subscriptions"
description: "Use SCP2P share links to subscribe quickly and connect to the right peers"
---

SCP2P supports compact share links in this format:

```text
scp2p://s/<payload>?bp=<bootstrap1,bootstrap2>
```

The link includes:

- The share ID
- The publisher share public key
- Optional bootstrap peer hints (`bp`) for first connection

## Subscribe from the desktop app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Discover">
    In the app sidebar, open **Discover**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add private share">
    Click **+** in the Discover header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste the link">
    Paste the full `scp2p://s/...` link and click **Subscribe**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sync">
    Click **Sync** to fetch the manifest immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the link contains bootstrap hints, add them in **Settings** under **Bootstrap Peers** if you cannot discover the share on first try.

## Subscribe from the CLI

Use the interactive CLI:

```bash
scp2p --bootstrap 203.0.113.10:7001
```

In the shell:

1. Open **Subscriptions**
2. Choose **Subscribe to a new share**
3. Paste the 64-byte share ID from the link
4. Paste the share public key when prompted
5. Run **Sync subscriptions now**

## Generate links for your own shares

In the desktop app:

1. Open **My Shares**
2. Click **Copy Link** on a share card
3. Send the link to the recipient

The app can include your current bind address and configured bootstrap peers as hints.

## Troubleshooting

- `No shares discovered`: verify both peers have nodes running.
- `Cannot sync`: confirm at least one reachable bootstrap peer in **Settings**.
- `Still empty after subscribe`: click **Sync** and then reopen the share in **Discover**.
